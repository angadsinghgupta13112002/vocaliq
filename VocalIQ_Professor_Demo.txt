--- OVERVIEW TAB ---

"This is the coaching report. At the top is an overall
score out of ten, and below that are five sub-scores —
content clarity, emotional delivery, vocal confidence,
pacing, and audience engagement.

Gemini detected the speaker's emotion and compared it
to what the scenario actually needs."

[POINT to the score ring and sub-scores]

--- EMOTION TIMELINE TAB ---

"This is the second new feature — the Cloud Vision
Emotion Timeline."

[CLICK: Emotion Timeline tab]

"The app extracted one frame every three seconds from
the video using the browser Canvas API, sent them to
Google Cloud Vision Face Detection, and mapped each
frame to an emotion.

The timeline bar shows the speaker's emotional state
second by second — green is confident, orange is
nervous, gray is neutral.

The summary cards show the dominant emotion, the
exact timestamps when the speaker looked nervous,
and when they looked confident.

Cloud Vision runs in parallel with Gemini so it
adds no extra time to the analysis."

[POINT to the timeline bar and summary cards]

--- TRANSCRIPT + TIPS (brief) ---

[CLICK: Transcript tab — pause 2 seconds]
[CLICK: Tips tab — pause 2 seconds]

"The Transcript tab shows the full timestamped
transcript Gemini produced. The Tips tab gives
targeted coaching exercises based specifically
on what Gemini observed — not generic advice."
