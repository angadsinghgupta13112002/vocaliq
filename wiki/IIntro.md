## What is VocalIQ?

VocalIQ is an AI-powered speaking coach built as a Single Page Application (SPA) using React, Node.js, and Express, deployed on Google Cloud Run. Users record or upload a speech video, optionally pick a video directly from Google Drive, and receive a full AI-generated coaching report in under 90 seconds.

The report includes:
- A 1–10 score with a 5-dimension breakdown (Content, Delivery, Confidence, Pacing, Engagement)
- A timestamped transcript with flagged issues and specific fixes
- A Cloud Vision emotion timeline showing frame-by-frame facial expressions and eye contact
- A gesture detection timeline powered by MediaPipe HandLandmarker
- Deep coaching tips, vocal control advice, and a personalized 7-day practice plan
